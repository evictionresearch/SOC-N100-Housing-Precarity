Week 5

Assignment 1 - Due Monday, August 4 @5pm

Choose a county or set of counties of interest and create a measure of displacement risk, inequality, or some other housing precarity measure using the American Community Survey. Include descriptive statistics, plots, and a brief interpretation of what you found. Submit to bCourses.

For this assignment you will submit:

  1. R code script that is replicable by the grader,

  2. a brief writeup explaining your assignment with plots and context,

    • Include one or more introductory paragraphs summarizing what you are analyzing and your overall findings.
    • Text and plots walking the reader through a more detailed understanding of what you found. Make sure to describe your plots.
    • Summary conclusion and anecdotal insights.

  3. Bibliography and inline citations in ASA (American Sociological Association) format.

  4. List of URL links to any and all AI usage (only use Perplexity.ai).

    • In your code, put the URL in comments next to the relevant area.
    • In your writeup, put the URL in footnotes near the section or text in which you used AI. See AI policy in the Syllabus for more info.

These assignments will be graded on the scale in the syllabus. This grade is to help you see the quality of your work but the final grade will be graded as complete or incomplete. No late assignments will be accepted except for special accommodations. You can volunteer to present a 5-minute demo of your assignment in class.
